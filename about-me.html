My Inspiration

My education background in healthcare administration has also contributed to my interest in software engineering. These skills are also valuable in software engineering, where teamwork and communication are essential for success.

Overall, my decision to become a software engineer is driven by my passion for technology, desire to make a difference, and the transferable skills I gained from my education and experience in healthcare administration. I'm eager to start this new chapter in my career and make an impact in the software engineering industry.
